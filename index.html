What I actually do

I'm a Search Engineer at Morgan Stanley, working across the full retrieval stack — ingestion pipelines, schema design, hybrid ranking, and the GenAI layer that turns search results into answers. The platform I help build serves 1.5M+ users globally, so most of my day is some mix of relevance tuning, reliability work, and figuring out why a query that should return one document returned zero.
